What Are We Having For Dinner?

random generator







This is an app to help you decide what to have for dinner. Click 'Pick Something' to get a random dinner item. If you like the option, you can save it to your list. Click 'See Options' to see all the options. You can delete and add items to this list to create your own custom list. We preloaded a few items into the list for you, so you can just have it 'pick something.' Click 'My List' to see all the options you've saved. You can use this to plan out dinners for the week (or longer).
